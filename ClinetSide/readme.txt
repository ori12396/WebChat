When our web app is running, you'll see the Log-in screen, where you can log in to the system with a hard-coded account or create your own account (You'll see a reference in the bottom of the log in screen)

If you have decided to create your own account, you can fill up a registration form and then you'll be re-directed back to the Log- in screen when you have completed the registartion and you can now log in with your new account

After Logging in, you'll see your nick name and your avatar on the top left of the chat screen, and next to them you could add a new chat with a friend. 
Below that, there is the list of your chats, Presenting the friend's avatar, his nick name, and the last message between you two.

At the right side of the screen there is the actual chat where you can read your conversation history and send many types of messages: Text message, A picture, A video, or even a voice message!

Features: 
Our Log-in screen, Registartion screen and pop-up modals are responding in REAL TIME, to give the user a feedback on his actions and help him out! 
Our modals are static so you cant accidently click outside of it and lose the stuff you were about to send!
